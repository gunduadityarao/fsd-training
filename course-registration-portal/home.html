CVR COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING
-Learn Today,Lead Tomorrow
CVR College of Engineering is a premier engineering institution located in Hyderabad, Telangana. Established in 2001, the college is affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering and technology disciplines. Known for its strong academic standards, modern infrastructure, experienced faculty, and excellent placement opportunities, CVR College emphasizes both technical excellence and holistic student development. The institution actively promotes research, innovation, industry collaboration, and extracurricular activities to prepare students for successful careers in engineering and technology.Short Version CVR College of Engineering is one of the leading engineering colleges in Hyderabad, offering quality education, state-of-the-art facilities, skilled faculty, and strong placement support. The college focuses on academic excellence, innovation, and overall student growth, helping students build successful careers in the technology and engineering sectors.
